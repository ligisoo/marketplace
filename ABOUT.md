# About Ligisoo

Welcome to Ligisoo, the premier marketplace connecting talented betting tipsters with a community of passionate sports fans.

## Our Mission

Our mission is to create a transparent and trustworthy ecosystem for sports betting tips. We empower expert tipsters by providing them with a platform to showcase their skills and monetize their knowledge. For our users, we offer a reliable source of high-quality, verified betting tips to help them make informed decisions.

## What We Do

Ligisoo is a dynamic marketplace where:

-   **Tipsters** can create and sell their sports betting tips. We provide tools to make this easy, including the ability to generate tips from betslip screenshots using our advanced OCR technology.
-   **Buyers** can browse a wide variety of tips from numerous tipsters, analyze their performance on our transparent leaderboards, and purchase the tips that best suit their needs.

## Our Commitment to Transparency

Trust is at the core of everything we do. We've built a robust system to ensure the integrity of our marketplace:

-   **Automated Verification:** Tip results are automatically verified using data from reliable sports APIs, ensuring that the win/loss records you see are accurate.
-   **Performance Leaderboards:** Our leaderboards provide a clear and unbiased view of each tipster's performance over time, helping you identify the most successful and consistent experts.
-   **Secure Payments:** We use Safaricom's M-Pesa for secure and reliable payment processing.

Whether you're a seasoned tipster looking to build a following or a sports enthusiast seeking an edge, Ligisoo is the platform for you.

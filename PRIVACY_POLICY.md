# Privacy Policy for Ligisoo

**Last Updated:** 2025-11-10

Welcome to Ligisoo ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. We will let you know of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date of this Privacy Policy.

**Disclaimer:** This is a template and not legal advice. You should consult with a legal professional to ensure this policy meets your specific needs and complies with all applicable laws.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways. The information we may collect on the platform includes:

### Personal Data
-   **Phone Number:** We collect your phone number for user registration, login, and for processing payments via M-Pesa.
-   **Email Address:** We may collect your email address if you voluntarily provide it. This may be used for communication purposes, such as sending notifications or responding to support queries.
-   **Profile Information:** You may choose to provide a display name and a profile picture for your public tipster profile.

### Transactional Data
-   **Purchase Information:** We keep a record of the tips you purchase and the tips you sell.
-   **Payment Information:** When you make a purchase, we process the payment through Safaricom's M-Pesa. We collect transaction details such as the M-Pesa transaction ID and receipt number to verify payments. We do not collect or store your M-Pesa PIN.
-   **Withdrawal Information:** For tipsters, we process withdrawals to your registered phone number.

### Technical Data
-   **Log and Usage Data:** We may automatically collect information when you access and use the application, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and the pages you visit. This is used for analytics and to improve the service.
-   **Device Data:** Information about the device you use to access our service.

### Data from Third Parties
-   **Sports Data:** We use third-party sports data APIs to automatically verify the results of matches. This data is not linked to your personal information.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
-   Create and manage your account.
-   Provide and operate our services, including the tip marketplace and leaderboards.
-   Process transactions and send you related information, including purchase confirmations and receipts.
-   Process withdrawals for tipsters.
-   Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience.
-   Communicate with you, including responding to your comments and questions.
-   Prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities.

## 3. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share information we have collected about you in certain situations:

### With Third-Party Service Providers

-   **Cloud Hosting:** Our application and data are hosted on cloud servers (e.g., AWS).
-   **Analytics Services:** We may share anonymized data with analytics providers to help us understand usage patterns.

### For Legal Reasons
We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).

# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 16, 2026

At Ligisoo, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with our platform and services.

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect the following types of information:
* **Account Information:** When you register, we collect your phone number (used as your primary identifier and for M-Pesa billing) and display name.
* **Usage Data:** We collect analytics data regarding your interactions with the platform, including pages visited, analysts followed, and subscription history.
* **Community Contributions:** Any opinions, analyses, or predictions you post on the platform are public and associated with your profile.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:
* Manage and authenticate your account.
* Process your subscription payments securely via M-Pesa.
* Improve our platform algorithms (e.g., ranking Top Analysts based on community engagement).
* Communicate important account updates or changes to our Terms of Service.

## 3. Data Protection and Compliance (ODPC)

Ligisoo operates in strict compliance with the **Kenya Data Protection Act**. 
* We act as a Data Controller for the personal information you provide.
* We implement robust security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
* Your data is not sold to third-party marketers.

## 4. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites. Please note that we are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of those external sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any site you visit.

## 5. Your Rights

Under the Data Protection Act, you have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact our support team.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@ligisoo.co.ke.
